Starfield

About the Project

The Starfield project is a visual simulation inspired by a moving starfield. Its functionality is based on the random generation of multiple points in space, representing stars moving toward the viewer.
Each point is assigned a random speed at the start of the program, which creates a sense of depth as some stars appear to move faster than others. This effect helps simulate a three-dimensional motion, similar to what one might experience when traveling through space at high speed.
When a point passes the viewer's perspective, meaning it crosses the foreground of the screen, it doesn't disappear. Instead, it is repositioned to the background, allowing it to move forward again in a continuous loop. This maintains a constant star density and reinforces the feeling of an infinite and dynamic universe.
