Poopy Walk

About the Game

Poopy Walk is a game developed during the Valencia Global Game Jam 2024. It is a 2D side-scrolling platformer with a humorous tone and accessible yet challenging mechanics.
The game begins with a tutorial level, designed to introduce the player to all the mechanics they will encounter throughout the experience, from basic movement to interacting with environmental obstacles and challenges. Once the tutorial is completed, the player can take on two main levels, where they must apply everything they've learned in order to successfully overcome them.

The objective is simple, yet quite peculiar: the player controls an office worker desperate to reach the bathroom before it's too late. There's a limited amount of time to get there, and the path is filled with obstacles and traps that will test the player's skills and sense of urgency.
With a mix of classic platforming, absurd humor, and unexpected situations, Poopy Walk delivers a fast-paced and entertaining experience, perfect for quick play sessions and for enjoying the kind of chaos that defines game jams.
