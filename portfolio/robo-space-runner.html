An Endless Runner

This represents an infinite runner game developed using Unity and released on the Google Play Store. The gameplay involves progressing through dynamically generated levels, evading obstacles, and gathering coins. The main objective of this endeavor was to gain insights into the publication procedures for platforms like the Google Play Store and the iOS App Store.

The project features a procedurally generated grid and autonomous entities capable of traversing this dynamically created environment, engaging with the grid's elements.

Features

  • Score System
  • Animations
  • Unity’s Particle System
  • Variety of skills (Triple Shoot, Shield, Tornado Kick & Invencibility)
