A GameCube Game made with DevkitPro and GRRLIB

Our game is called Toy Tanks. It's about two tanks that must destroy each other to win. To destroy each other they use two weapons, the first one is a tank gun and the second one is the bomb called boombs.

Each player has five lifes, every time a tank is hit loses a life, when a tank runs out of lifes the game will end and the other tank will win the match.

For this project we have worked with the devkitpro and the GRRLIB library. These tools are really useful because you can work on many different consoles without having to own the console, it is also great due to its easy use. Despite we have used them to work for the Gamecube it also works on Wii.
