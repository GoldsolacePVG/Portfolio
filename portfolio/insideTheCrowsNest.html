Inside The Crow's Nest

About the Game

Inside the Crow's Nest is a dark fantasy survival horror game in which the player explores the corridors of an ancient monastery, corrupted by pagan rituals, hidden horrors, and the fanaticism of a cult that controls everything. Developed by the Crimson Covenant team as a final-year project, the game delivers an intense, atmospheric experience that pays homage to the classics of the genre.

Taking on the role of a prisoner of the cult, the player must escape the monastery by solving puzzles, overcoming challenges, and facing enemies, while avoiding being recaptured. Throughout the escape, the player is armed with a crossbow, their only means of defense against the cult's followers, which can be used strategically to break through obstacles or eliminate direct threats.

The game draws direct inspiration from the survival horror titles of the original PlayStation era, reflected in both gameplay and visual style. Among its most distinctive features are tank-style controls, which enhance tension and force careful movement planning, and a “PSX” visual mode, which applies retro and pixelated filters to emulate the polygonal aesthetics of classic games.

Inside the Crow's Nest aims not only to evoke fear but also nostalgia, offering a dense, oppressive experience that remains faithful to the roots of the genre while incorporating modern mechanics and real-time narrative design.
